A vegetarian journey into richness and elegance. Shiitake mushrooms plucked from oaken forests. These are raised to unbelievable heights of sophistication--bathed in cream and robed in fresh herbs and seasonings. It's a soup fit for Kubla Khan and his stately pleasure-dome in Xanadu

This Italian vegetable soup with pasta is packed with flavour & ingredients! Tomatoes, carrots, kidney beans, potatoes, Italian green beans, spinach, celery, peas and zucchini harmoniously fill a soup bowl with colour and appetite. Hearty-Soup Nutsy style.

Chilled Soups

Andalusian Gazpacho
Our own popular creation. Traditional ingredients including Spanish red onions, garlic, jalapeno, sweet red pepper & cucumber. We added just enough luscious lime & lemon to create a thirst quenching chilled summer soup that will please everyone. Delicious…Refreshing… Nutritious…
Avocado & Cucumber Soup
The combination of English cucumbers and avocadoes with the right amount of spice with and a hint of cilantro blended in a rich cream, makes this chilled soup a summer favourite. Refreshing and satisfying!
Citrus - Shrimp Gazpacho
A different twist to our regular gazpacho. The citrus flavours are highlighted with sweet baby shrimp, crisp vegetables and a few selected spices. Chill out this summer with a truly refreshing soup.
